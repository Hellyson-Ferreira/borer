Borer

Drill through NATs. Own your tunnels.

Borer is a self-hosted tunneling system. It exposes local services to the internet by drilling through NATs and firewalls using persistent tunnels.

The server does not execute requests. All traffic is forwarded to a local agent, keeping your infrastructure minimal and your services private.
